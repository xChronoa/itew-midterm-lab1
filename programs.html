Programs (9)

Stretching

A practice involving deliberate lengthening and loosening of muscles to enhance flexibility and prevent injury.

Warm Up

Preparatory exercises aimed at gradually increasing heart rate, blood flow, and body temperature before engaging in more strenuous physical activity.

Cardio

Physical exercises, such as running, cycling, or swimming, designed to strengthen the cardiovascular system and improve overall endurance.

Chest

A series of resistance exercises targeting the pectoral muscles, commonly including bench presses, chest flies, and cable crossovers.

Back

A regimen focusing on strengthening the muscles of the back, involving exercises such as pull-ups, rows, and deadlifts.

Shoulder

A workout routine designed to develop the deltoid muscles and enhance shoulder strength and stability, incorporating movements like shoulder presses, lateral raises, and upright rows.

Legs

A set of exercises aimed at building lower body strength and muscle mass, comprising squats, lunges, leg presses, and calf raises.

Triceps

A program emphasizing the development of the triceps muscles, involving exercises like tricep dips, tricep extensions, and close-grip bench presses.

Biceps

A workout regimen targeting the biceps muscles to increase arm strength and size, typically including bicep curls, hammer curls, and chin-ups.
